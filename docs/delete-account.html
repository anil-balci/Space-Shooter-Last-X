Delete Account & Data – LastX: Space Shooter

If you would like to delete your account and associated data for LastX: Space Shooter (com.spaceshooter.lastx), please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to ianilbalci@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete Account – LastX”.
  2. In the email, include the login method you used (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, or Guest account).
  3. We will verify your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What will be deleted

  • Your game account and authentication profile (Firebase Auth).
  • Progress and in-app purchase history stored under your account.
  • Any associated analytics data tied to your account ID.

What will be retained

Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory requirements (e.g., financial transaction records for purchases via Google Play or Apple App Store). Such data is kept securely and only as long as required by law.

Contact

If you have questions, please email ianilbalci@gmail.com.

This page is provided to comply with Google Play’s account deletion policy and does not constitute legal advice.
