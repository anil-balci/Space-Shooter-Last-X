Privacy Policy

App: LastX: Space Shooter (com.spaceshooter.lastx)
Last updated: 2025-08-27

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game LastX: Space Shooter collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app on Android or iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. If you have questions, contact us at ianilbalci@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and basic profile information from those providers. Guest accounts are supported but are anonymous.
  • Device and usage data: Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID, Apple Identifier for Advertisers), app version, language, country, and gameplay events are collected via Firebase Analytics and AdMob.
  • Crash and diagnostics data: Firebase Crashlytics may collect crash logs and performance metrics.
  • Purchases: If you make an in-app purchase, payments are processed by:
    • Google Play Billing (Android)
    • Apple App Store In-App Purchases (iOS)
    We receive purchase confirmations (e.g., product ID, transaction ID) but no payment card details.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide guest login, Google/Apple sign-in, and manage in-app purchases on both Android and iOS.
  • Operate and improve game features, gameplay balance, and stability.
  • Serve advertisements, including personalized ads where you have consented.
  • Monitor crashes, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third-party providers that may act as independent controllers of your data:

  • Google AdMob – advertising: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase (Auth, Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config): https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases on Android: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / In-App Purchases – in-app purchases on iOS: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with service providers (e.g., Google, Firebase, Apple) or disclosed if required by law.

6. Children’s Privacy

This game is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have done so in error, please contact us.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings or Google Ads Settings.
  • You may reset your advertising ID (Android) or Identifier for Advertisers (iOS) in your device’s settings.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us at ianilbalci@gmail.com.

9. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers outside your country of residence. Our providers implement safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions, requests, or concerns, please email us at ianilbalci@gmail.com.
